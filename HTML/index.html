AN INVITATION
 
6 September 2017  •  New World Makati Hotel
 
FIS cordially invites you to attend the Financial Inclusion executive roundtable on 6 September.

Held in conjunction with the FIS Connect Manila, this invitation-only senior executive dialogue on financial inclusion will be attended by C level bankers and senior representatives of Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas, and will examine the business strategies, technology and partnerships required to bring modern, innovative financial services to the underbanked and unbanked.

We hope you can join us in this engaging session. Kindly RSVP to Vida Bayanin before 28 August or 63 91 7863955 to attend. As lunch will be served during the roundtable, may we request if you can specify diet restrictions for lunch.

Prior to joining the Financial Inclusion roundtable, may we also invite you to join the FIS Connect Manila opening sessions which will be held from 9 to 10.30 am. For more information and registration for FIS Connect Manila, visit here.

FEATURED SPEAKERS
 
 
 
Chandra Shekhar Ghosh
Managing Director and CEO, Bandhan Bank
 
Chandra Shekhar Ghosh is the founder, Managing Director and CEO of Bandhan Bank, the first instance of a microfinance institution becoming a universal bank in India. With 25 years of experience in the field of microfinance and development, he is a key mentor in the microfinance sector, is a senior Ashoka Fellow and sits on several boards and committees for financial inclusion. For his contributions, he has been recognized with the Forbes India Leadership award for Entrepreneur with Social Impact, the CNN-News 18 award for Business and the Entrepreneur of the Year by Ernst & Young. In addition, he has won numerous awards for his work in microfinance, for management excellence and outstanding leadership in financial services.
 
 
 
Michael Araneta
Associate Vice President and Research Director,
IDC Financial Insights APAC
 
Michael Araneta is Research Director for IDC Financial Insights in APAC and country manager for IDC Thailand. He leads the APAC financial services practice and is a thought leader across a broad range of strategic and tactical issues in Asian banking. Araneta has consulted for banks in innovative and transformative projects. He is driving financial inclusion and microfinance discussions around the region to extend the penetration of financial services.
