FIS is excited to announce the FIS Connect Manila 2017 – a one day event featuring impactful insight on trends in the financial industry. Join FIS and industry experts and explore challenges facing banks in the new economy.

  MORE INFO
 
 
  Imagine Possibilities
As banks face disruption from several fronts, it is crucial to understand how innovation and disruption impact the customer experience. At the event, industry leaders and FIS solution experts and executives will share ideas and best practices for today’s challenging environment.

Save the date to attend – registration details will follow shortly – to join the conversation on a broad range of topics including digital transformation, open API banking, card and fraud management, core modernization, real-time payments and financial inclusion.

Keynote speaker Dan Cobley is an entrepreneur and investor in innovative tech startup businesses, focusing on financial services. Prior to this, he spent almost a decade at Google, serving as Managing Director, UK & Ireland and as Vice President of EMEA Marketing. Dan will address the disruptive power of fintech and innovation and how digital connectivity and devices are transforming consumer behaviour and the industries that depend on them. He shares lessons learned from Google’s unique approach to business and how they can be applied to other companies and sectors.
